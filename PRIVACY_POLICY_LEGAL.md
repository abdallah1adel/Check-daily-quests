# Privacy Policy for PCPOS Companion

**Last Updated**: November 27, 2025

## Introduction

PCPOS Companion ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Biometric Data (Optional)
- **Face Recognition Data**: If you enroll in our biometric system, we capture and store facial feature embeddings (mathematical representations, not actual photos)
- **Voice Recognition Data**: If you enroll in voice recognition, we store voice embeddings (mathematical representations, not actual recordings)
- **Storage**: Biometric data is encrypted and stored both locally on your device (iOS Keychain) and in our secure cloud database (Firebase/Firestore)

### 2. Account Information
- **Email Address**: For account creation and authentication
- **Display Name**: Your chosen username
- **Profile Photo**: If you choose to upload one or link from social media

### 3. Social Media Data (Optional)
- **Facebook**: If you link your Facebook account, we receive your name, email, and profile picture
- **Instagram**: If you link Instagram, we may receive your username and profile information

### 4. Usage Data
- **Analytics**: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how you use the app
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash logs to improve app stability

## How We Use Your Information

- **Personalization**: To customize your AI companion experience
- **Authentication**: To verify your identity
- **Multi-User Features**: To enable personalized experiences across devices
- **Service Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve the app

## Data Storage & Security

- **Encryption**: All biometric data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
- **Cloud Storage**: We use Google Firebase (SOC 2 compliant) for cloud storage
- **Local Storage**: Sensitive data is stored in iOS Keychain
- **Access Control**: Only you can access your biometric data

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase**: Authentication, database, and analytics
- **Facebook SDK**: For Facebook Login (optional)
- **Apple Services**: Face ID, Speech Recognition (processed on-device)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data at any time
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your data
- **Opt-out** of biometric features
- **Revoke** social media connections
