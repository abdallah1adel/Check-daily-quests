Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Introduction

PCPOS Companion ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

1. Biometric Data (Optional)

  • Face Recognition Data: Mathematical representations of facial features (not actual photos)
  • Voice Recognition Data: Mathematical voice embeddings (not actual recordings)
  • Storage: Encrypted locally (iOS Keychain) and in secure cloud (Firebase)

2. Account Information

  • Email address for authentication
  • Display name
  • Profile photo (optional)

3. Social Media Data (Optional)

  • Facebook: Name, email, profile picture (if you link your account)
  • Instagram: Username and profile information (if linked)

4. Usage Data

  • Firebase Analytics for app improvement
  • Anonymous crash reports

How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization of your AI companion experience
  • User authentication and verification
  • Multi-user features across devices
  • Service improvement and analytics

Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption: All biometric data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Cloud Storage: Google Firebase (SOC 2 compliant)
  • Local Storage: iOS Keychain for sensitive data
  • Access Control: Only you can access your biometric data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of biometric features
  • Revoke social media connections
