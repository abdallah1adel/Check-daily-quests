# PCPOS Companion - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025

## Welcome

PCPOS Companion ("the App") is an AI-powered companion app that provides personalized interactions through voice, vision, and text. Your privacy is important to us.

## Information We Collect

### Locally Stored Data
- **Voice Recordings**: Used only for on-device speech recognition. Not uploaded or stored permanently.
- **Camera Feed**: Processed locally for emotion detection. Not recorded or uploaded.
- **User Preferences**: Animation settings, personality parameters, voice selection
- **Chat History**: Stored locally on your device
- **User Traits**: Learned preferences and interaction patterns (stored locally)

### Data Sent to Third Parties

When you subscribe to **PCPOS Pro**, the following data may be sent to third-party services:

#### OpenAI (Chat Completion)
- **Data Sent**: Your text messages and conversation history
- **Purpose**: To provide AI-powered responses
- **Retention**: According to OpenAI's data retention policy
- **Privacy Policy**: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

#### ElevenLabs (Optional - Voice Synthesis)
- **Data Sent**: Text to be converted to speech
- **Purpose**: High-quality voice generation
- **Retention**: According to ElevenLabs' data retention policy
- **Privacy Policy**: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy

## How We Use Your Information

- **Personalization**: To tailor the companion's personality and responses to your preferences
- **Service Improvement**: To enhance AI accuracy and user experience
- **Subscription Management**: To verify and manage in-app purchases

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Your identity or contact information (unless voluntarily provided)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Persistent camera recordings
- ❌ Data from other apps

## Your Rights

- **Access**: View your locally stored data through the app's Settings
- **Deletion**: Clear chat history and reset user traits at any time
- **Opt-Out**: Use the app without Pro subscription to avoid third-party data sharing

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored until you delete the app or manually clear data
- **Third-Party Data**: Subject to each service's retention policy (OpenAI, ElevenLabs)

## Subscription Data

- **Apple handles all payment processing**: We do not store credit card information
- **Subscription Status**: Verified through Apple's StoreKit framework

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of updates.
